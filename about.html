About Us

Welcome to RainyDays, where we believe in pushing the comfort zone for outdoor enthusiasts. Our passion lies in equipping men and women aged 30 to 50 with durable rain jackets designed for all their adventurous pursuits.

Whether it's hiking through rugged terrain, skiing down snowy slopes, or camping under the stars, our mid-range jackets are crafted to withstand diverse weather conditions while ensuring ultimate comfort.

Our Story

Our story began on a stormy night during a hiking trip in the Pacific Northwest. Founders Sarah and Mike, avid adventurers themselves, found their expedition cut short by inadequate gear. Inspired by this experience, they set out to create a brand that ensures no adventure is hindered by the elements. Thus, RainyDays was born. Their vision was to develop high-quality, reliable, and stylish rain jackets that could withstand the harshest weather conditions and keep adventurers comfortable and protected.
